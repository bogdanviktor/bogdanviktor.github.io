About Us

Unique features of our platform

Yojee is quickly building a delivery network across the Asia-Pacific region with global expansion plans through the power of the collaborative economy and the participation of respected and trusted carriers seeking access to additional revenue and world class technology. We bring a platform that is managing deliveries at scale, using a technology backend including APIs so powerful that small to medium businesses can pull parcel capacity off the Yojee grid to enable greater revenue along big data optimised routes which are constantly becoming more efficient through machine learning. Senders support businesses struggling against the big guys and carriers get access to revenue and technology which has traditionally been held by those they historically could not compete with.
