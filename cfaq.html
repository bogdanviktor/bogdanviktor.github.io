Sending with Yojee - FAQs

How do I send a parcel or document using Yojee?

You can create a delivery order by using Yojee’s Sender app (free to download from Android and Apple iOS stores) or via the web at http://order.sendyojee.com (Singapore only).

What are Yojee’s delivery fees?

Yojee endeavours to be transparent with our pricing so you don’t feel hit with unexpected ‘hidden charges’. Our three-hour express pricing is available here, and our same day delivery pricing is available here. Yojee currently charges delivery fees based on weight of your parcel coupled with distance and timing window of a delivery job. Kindly note there may be surcharges (e.g. for ERP). The final fee will be displayed at booking confirmation. For full details, please see our three-hour express and same day pricing.

How do I create a Yojee Sender account?

You do not need to create a Yojee account to start sending parcels, although require both Sender and Recipient's details (in the unlikely event that we may need to contact you) and Sender’s credit card details (for secure payment). Just download Yojee’s Sender app (Android or iOS) or go to http://order.sendyojee.com (Singapore only) and you’re good to Yojee your parcels safely to their destination!

How do I use Yojee’s Sender app?

The Yojee Sender App can be downloaded for free from either Android or Apple iOS app stores. We pride ourselves on making our software beautiful and simple to use. If you need more help, please contact Yojee’s Customer Support at cs@sendyojee.com or at +65 3159 1335.

What are Yojee’s delivery times?

Yojee currently delivers between 10am – 10pm, Monday through Saturday. Our Customer Support team will be available to help you throughout those times.

How do I pay for deliveries?

Yojee currently accepts payment via credit card (Visa, Mastercard, American Express).

Which credit cards does Yojee accept?

Yojee accepts payment via Visa, Mastercard and American Express.

What kind of items can I deliver using Yojee?

Currently, Yojee delivers any item up to 40kg in weight and 58cm x 58cm x 58cm in dimension. Yojee cares about the safety of our Delivery Partners so please ensure that items are not on our prohibited items list which can be found in our T&Cs.

Are there any areas in Singapore that Yojee does not deliver to?

Yojee delivers anywhere in Singapore except surrounding islands, military camps and bases, and the Istana.

May I select the timing of when my parcels are collected and/or delivered?

Yojee currently offers a three-hour premium delivery window and a same-day delivery service. The premium three-hour service starts the moment you confirm your order. Should you require very specific time as an exception for, say, a birthday surprise or an urgent document delivery, please contact Yojee’s Customer Support at cs@sendyojee.com or at +65 3159 1335 and we will do our very best to help!

May I send multiple parcels, picked up from one location then sent to various locations?

You may send as many parcels as you wish, whether pickup or delivery is from one or multiple locations. Each parcel for delivery must be input as a separate, new order for it to have its own tracking number. For bulk delivery orders, please contact Yojee’s Customer Support at cs@sendyojee.com or at +65 3159 1335.

Do I need to pack or wrap my items?

Yojee wants to ensure that your parcels arrive safely to their destination. We kindly request that you package contents carefully to prevent them from being damaged during transit. Yojee’s Delivery Partners are trained to handle goods with utmost care but please note that Yojee will not be held liable for damage resulting from items being improperly and/or insufficiently padded and packaged.

How do I track my parcel?

Yojee’s Sender apps (both mobile and web) offer live tracking of your parcel from the moment it is picked up by our Delivery Partner.

I received notification that my parcel has been successfully delivered but the recipient says they have not received it. What do I do?

In the rare event of a problem with your delivery please contact Yojee’s Customer Support at +65 3159 1335 immediately.

Are my items insured?

Yes, Yojee insures for items up to S$500 in value per delivery order. Should you require additional insurance coverage for your parcels, please contact Yojee’s Customer Support at cs@sendyojee.com or at +65 3159 1335.

How will I know that my parcel has been delivered?

Yojee wants to ensure that parcels are delivered safely to the correct recipient so we require a signature proof-of-delivery (POD) by the recipient named on the order. Once this has occurred, you will receive an SMS confirming that delivery has been made.

I am now unable to be at the pickup / drop-off location. What do I do?

Yojee’s Sender App provides a countdown timer for you to know when our Delivery Partner will be picking up or dropping off your parcel. Yojee’s Delivery Partners work to efficient schedules so should a Sender or Recipient fail to meet a Partner Driver within ten minutes of the designated pick-up time, please be aware that Yojee will charge 50% of the delivery fee. We kindly ask that you reschedule the pickup or delivery by placing a new order.

Can parcels be left by a front-door or anywhere else?

Yojee’s Delivery Partners are unable to confirm delivery of parcels without a signature proof-of-delivery (POD) by the named recipient. You may choose to provide explicit written consent (SMS to the Delivery Partner or email Yojee’s Customer Support at cs@sendyojee.com authorising someone else to sign the POD or instructing for the parcel to be left outside but Yojee will not be held liable for parcels that subsequently go missing.

How do I contact Yojee?

You can contact Yojee’s Customer Support at cs@sendyojee.com or at +65 3159 1335. We are contactable during Yojee’s delivery operating hours, 10am – 10pm, Monday through Saturday.
